Competitions

The competitions at​ Abhyuday, IIT Bombay​ is a platform to provide a sustainable solution model to the ​social problems ​by engaging with the community and stakeholders.​ ​Aimed at encouraging youth towards solving social issues with their innovativeness, ​Competitions, has ​covered about​ 25+ states ​and ​45+ cities ​with the participation of​ 800+ teams ​including over ​2000 students ​& Professionals from around​ 1500 colleges ​across the nation till now​.

Action Plan

A nationwide competition to find India's most promising social entrepreneurs. Action plan, the flagship competition of Abhyuday aimed at raising awareness about social entrepreneurship, Action Plan motivates the youth to solve India's most pressing social issues. Action-Plan, which revolves around the 3I’s mantra: Ideate – Innovate – Implement, provides the platform to the youngsters across the country to come up with feasible and optimistic solutions to tackle social issues present in the Indian society. Started in 2012, it has rapidly expanded and last year reached to around 700+ teams of 2000+ different colleges in 50+ cities across India

Kalamanch

In its 2017-inaugural run, the aim of Kalamanch was to convey the multifaceted themes in fighting the social problems which are related to human rights, thereby condemning the deterioration of human rights by raising awareness among one and all through art and photography, consisting of paintings, photographs, graffiti, digital art and different artworks. It stood a major attraction to visitors.

Adhikaar

The Battle of Words One of the largest national level inter-college debate competitions organized at IIT Bombay, Adhikaar’s themes are based on some of the burning topics on various sectors such as violation of human rights, women harassment, governance, education, etc. In the fest organised in 2018, it happened to attract more than 70+ teams from differents colleges making it a huge success in its very first year.

PIL

A nationwide competition with the motivation to promote student involvement in laws and policies related to social issues by providing a fair and competitive environment for an exchange of thought and to strive and develop participant’s knowledge related to them to bring positive change in the society directly by involving law and support.
